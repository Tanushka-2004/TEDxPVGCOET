What is TEDx?

TEDx PROGRAM →

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxPVGCOET, where x = independently organized TED event. TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. TED provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual events like ours are self-organized.

TEDx PVGCOET 2024

PUNARUTTHAN

TEDxPVGCOET 2024 marked the fourth edition of TEDx Talks at PVG's College of Engineering and Technology, Pune, held on January 13th, 2024. Centered around the theme "Punarutthan", which means "Revival" or "Reawakening", the event aimed to spark conversations around rediscovering purpose, identity, and collective progress.

With a diverse lineup of speakers from renowned YouTuber Nomad Shubham to classical artist Dr. Swati Daithankar and cybersecurity expert Lt. Col. Dr. Santosh Khadsare – TEDxPVGCOET brought together voices from travel, music, entrepreneurship, archaeology, literature, and more. The event celebrated the power of ideas to revive, rebuild, and reimagine a better future for society.

TED empowers communities by giving a voice to ideas worth spreading.

TEDx is a locally organized program that brings communities together to share a TED-like experience. Licensed by TED but independently run, these events feature a mix of live speakers and TED Talk videos, all aimed at inspiring deep conversations and fresh ideas. Rooted in the spirit of “ideas worth spreading,” TEDx events celebrate innovation, storytelling, and connection giving a platform to voices that spark curiosity, challenge norms, and encourage change within local communities.
